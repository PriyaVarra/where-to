<h1 class="info-header">Where To?</h1>
<p><strong>Planning a road trip? Enter the amount of time you want to spend on the road and we&rsquo;ll find interesting attractions for you!</strong></p>
<h3 class="info-header">Using Where To</h3>
<p>1. Under <em>Home Location&nbsp;</em>there are two options for setting your starting location: &nbsp<em>Use My Location &nbsp</em>and&nbsp;<em>Use Address</em>. To use the first option, location permissions will need to be enabled for your browser.</p>
<p>2. Enter your desired travel time in hours and minutes under&nbsp;<em>Travel Time</em></p>
<p>3. Under <em>Destination Type&nbsp;</em> you can select the type of attractions you would like to visit. 
<p>4. Click&nbsp;<em>Search&nbsp;</em>to get your results! You can view each attraction by clicking the pins on the map and viewing the infocards.</p>
<h3 class="info-header">Saving Attractions</h3>
<p> After making a search, you can save any of the resulting attractions to view them again at a later time. To save an attraction, click the star icon on the corresponding infocard. </p>
<p> For this feature, you will need to be logged into an account which you can create by clicking <em>Sign Up&nbsp;</em> on the top right corner of the page </p>
